Background

Mazda is dedicated to enriching the customer experience at every touchpoint. Our goal is lifelong customer satisfaction, driven by these 3 core values.

Radically human

We place the human being at the center of everything we do: mind, body and spirit. We seek to stimulate the mind with human-centric design, care for the body through safety and comfort, and feed the spirit with the excitement that only a Mazda can offer.

Challenger spirit

Coming from Hiroshima, we take pride in our heritage, using it as inspiration to tackle challenges limitlessly. Every obstacle becomes an opportunity for innovation, fueling our determination and bold approach to future challenges.

Omotenashi

We value each customer uniquely, aiming to create personalised experiences and exceeding customer expectations at every step of their Mazda journey. Hospitality, for us, goes beyond gestures—it’s the essence of how we treat those we serve.

Challenge

How can we effectively communicate these 3 values through the Mazda website and build the long-lasting relationship?
